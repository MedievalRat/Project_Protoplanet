Eclipses: Perspective is Everything


Imagine a perfectly normal day, scrambled eggs with a vacado toast topped with a ncie cold glass of milk waiting on the countertop, asking to be eaten. The sun is slowly rising and everyone is waking from their deep slumber. You hear the early birds chirp and see sparkly dew settle on the various blades of grass. Suddenly, you feel colder, your gut clenches in anticipation. Fear hits you like a bullet. Boom! Everything is pitch black. It's like time reversed and it's night again. The sun simply vanished! Well, this phenomenom is called an eclipse. An eclipse is when a celestial body is obsurced by being in the shadow of another celestial body. Let's go through the story of eclipses.
